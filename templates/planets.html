some thoughts

the planets for these were derived from photos i took at the natural history museum, of their various rocks and such .

it was a trip i made for my a level art project . i then filtered and liquified in photoshop to make them look more spherical and enchanting

i wanted here to play around with making elementary shaders . for me it's pretty cool as its making the paintings and photoshops into something that moves , and something you can interact with !

planets in orbit

the first one - wanted to seem like planets were going above and below . multiple experiments, first had planets bouncing off which looked sort of uncanny. then decided to make it 'look' like the planets were going above and below . then added shading . had a lot of trouble with the weird switch when the planet moved past the sun - never figured out how to fix this . it is a weird 2d thing, but i like it a lot

experiments with masking

the second and third consist of explorations with masking in p5

wavy, masked planets

the third i grabbed a bunch of waves to make a somewhat random line for the planets to bob up and down in .

gangam style

the last one i refer to as 'gangnam style' as when i play the song whilst watching the animation, it does kinda seem like they are moving in beat. this was an accident caused by centering the canvas at the wrong point causing everything to move erratically but i enjoy the chaos of it . i placed all the planet textures on this one because why not !
